Vanderbilt University Medical Center

Vanderbilt University Medical Center’s Office of News & Communications provides media access to the newsmakers and opinion leaders at the medical center. The team at VUMC was looking to redesign its existing Department of News & Comm website to help improve its structure and overall user experience. I handled the design process, from initial wireframes to final designs and development prep. Today, the News & Comm site averages over 20,000 viewers a month, and provides media relations experts important resources for one of the country’s largest academic medical centers.
