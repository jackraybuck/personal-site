Raybuck Release One

Raybuck is my freelance art and design practice where I feel completely free to explore any and all interests, curiosities, and conceptual ideas. Having a separate space outside of any full-time job for my own personal work has always been important to me, and Raybuck is the formal expression of that desire. The products sold in the shop exist in parallel to the design work done at Raybuck, and allow for greater freedom of expression and exploration. This first collection was meant to cover a breadth of ideas and interests between 2022 and 2024.
