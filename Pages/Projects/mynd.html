Mynd

Mynd is a mobile app that makes conscious living easier for its users. In a world filled with distractions, Mynd makes keeping track of what’s going on in our daily lives simple. The app allows users to plan out days, set goals and intentions based on how they want to spend their waking hours, and track their progress against how they think they’re spending their days. The purpose of the app is to give users control of their lives, utilizng their most used piece of technology (their phone) to stay on top of what they’re doing day-to-day.
