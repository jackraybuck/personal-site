Lucky Fish

Lucky Fish is a concept for an Artisanal Tea House based in Kansas City, Missouri. Positioned as a higher-end tea shop, the Lucky Fish brand communicates quality product combined with a more sophisticated experience and personality. Type, color, and iconography all pull from the depths of the ocean (per a project brief) and work together to present its customers a product offering that is unmatched in the city.
