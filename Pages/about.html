About Me

My name is Jack Raybuck, and I’m a graphic designer and artist living in Phoenix, Arizona.

I like to help people tell their stories by creating and developing brand identities, brand strategies, and visual languages. I’m at my best when I’m finding ways to combine my marketing education with my experience as an Art Director.

I'm interested in:

  • Collaboration
  • Working across various mediums
  • Failing (and learning) fast and often
  • Following my intuition
  • Finding new ways to express myself

When I’m not working, I like to read, write, draw, and spend time in the sun.
